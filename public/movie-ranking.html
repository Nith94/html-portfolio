The Best Movies According to Nithin

My top 3 movies of all-time

Thanmatra

This movie captures the raw emotions of a man who has alzheimers and how his family deals with his illness.

Forever My Girl

This movie capture the emotions of a man who lost his way in life after his mother's tragic death and his fear of building relationship due to the possibility of losing his loved ones.

Top Gun

This deals with the adventurous life of a fighter pilot
